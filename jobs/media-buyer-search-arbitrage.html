Media Buyer Search Arbitrage

Remote

Full - Time

2 years

At Adsorb we help our clients achieve the efficiency and ROI they need to succeed. We are currently searching for a talented and motivated Search Arbitrage Media Buyer to become a crucial part of our team.

About the Job

As a Search Arbitrage Media Buyer, you will play a pivotal role in creating, managing, and scaling search arbitrage campaigns across multiple platforms. Your strong analytical skills and creative thinking will drive the highest possible return on investment (ROI) for our clients. This is an opportunity to independently design, set up, monitor, and optimize campaigns while actively identifying growth opportunities and adapting strategies to respond to market trends. Your work will be diverse, challenging, and detail-oriented, making you an integral part of our success.

‍

About you

As a motivated and results-driven media buyer, you possess a natural talent for detail and a deep interest in performance marketing. You understand the importance of performance metrics and can utilize them to continuously assess and improve campaign effectiveness. Your ability to work efficiently across various platforms and tools showcases your tech-savviness. Additionally, your excellent copywriting skills allow you to create persuasive ad copies that captivate our target audience.

‍

Perks of working at Adsorb:

  • As part of our team, you’ll enjoy an environment that fosters freedom, creativity, and impact.
  • You’ll have the autonomy to drive your own projects, design, build, and run campaigns according to your vision.
  • You will have the flexibility of remote work, allowing you to work from the comfort of your preferred location.
  • You’ll work alongside an international team of talented professionals, creating an enriching and collaborative experience.
  • We believe in recognizing and rewarding exceptional performance, and our bonus system reflects this commitment.
  • We provide ample opportunities for growth and career advancement, allowing you to achieve your professional goals.

‍

Responsibilities

  • Create, manage, and scale search arbitrage campaigns to maximize performance and ROI.
  • Utilize strong analytical skills to conduct research and identify promising verticals for testing.
  • Independently set up, monitor, and optimize media campaigns across various platforms.
  • Continuously monitor campaign performance, making strategic adjustments to ensure optimal results.
  • Proactively identify opportunities for campaign growth and adapt strategies to respond to market trends.
  • Utilize performance metrics to assess and improve campaign effectiveness.
  • Collaborate with the team to develop and implement A/B testing methodologies for high-converting landing pages.
  • Craft compelling ad copies that drive engagement and conversions, leveraging excellent copywriting skills.
